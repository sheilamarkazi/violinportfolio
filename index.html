Fiddle Player
Violin Luthier

Sheila is a fiddle player and a violin luthier. With a background in engineering and biology, her work is inspired by the structure and beauty of natural forms. Traveling often, she enjoys playing and teaching fiddle in the different communities she finds herself in. She currently resides in Mount Airy, North Carolina, soaking in the Round Peak music while studying violin making with Joseph A. Thrift.
